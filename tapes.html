TAPES テープ
The story of AZY — told across six recovered tapes.
RECOVERED FOOTAGE 回収映像
TAPE 001 起源 — ORIGIN

アジーは着陸しました

Azy is an interdimensional being who arrived on Earth accidentally, as if he had crossed a layer of reality without realizing it. This displacement caused a rupture in his own consciousness, making him lose his memory completely

TAPE 002 覚醒 — AWAKENING

Upon arriving, he begins to exist in a constant state of reflection. He sees the world, but does not understand its meanings. This leads Azy not only to observe, but to question and try to understand everything around him.

TAPE 003 悟り — REALIZATION

ウミは人間ができることなら、何でもできた。

The videos represent fragments of this existence. They are not linear records, but pieces of moments where Azy tries to integrate into society. These fragments do not explain his story, but instead show his states of presence, reflection, inner connection, and learning。

When you're near AZY, things become clear.

TAPE 004 旅立ち — DEPARTURE

In parallel to the life he begins to build, Azy also starts trying to understand himself. At different moments, he attempts to connect his experiences on Earth with possible fragments of who he once was. As he observes and learns, he begins to question his role there.

What he was looking for — nobody knew. Not even AZY himself.

TAPE 005 旅路 — THE JOURNEY

In the end, his journey is divided between two things happening at the same time: living a new life on Earth and trying to understand what it means for himself.

AZY traveled across the country. Never stopping. Never looking back. Quietly changing lives wherever he went.
