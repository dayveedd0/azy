FEES
Full transparency on how creator fees support $AZY.

$AZY

$AZY's creator fees exist for one purpose: to sustain and grow this project. Every dollar collected is allocated toward initiatives that directly benefit the token and its community. There are no hidden wallets, no undisclosed allocations. Below is a complete breakdown of how fees are utilized.

01

TIKTOK ADS & MARKETING

A significant portion of creator fees is invested into paid advertising — primarily TikTok campaigns — alongside broader marketing efforts. This includes influencer partnerships, social media promotion, community outreach, and targeted campaigns across crypto and mainstream audiences. The objective is to maximize $AZY's visibility and drive consistent organic growth.

02

CONTENT CREATION

Quality content is the backbone of any strong community. Fees fund the production of original videos, memes, graphics, and media that keep the $AZY brand active and engaging across all platforms. Consistent, high-quality content builds trust and attracts new holders organically.

03

TRENDING

A portion of fees is allocated toward getting $AZY listed on trending pages across major platforms — DEXScreener, DEXTools, CoinGecko, and others. Trending placement provides organic exposure to thousands of active traders and investors, making it one of the most effective growth strategies available.

04

BUYBACKS

Fees are used to purchase $AZY directly from the open market. Buybacks create consistent buy pressure, reinforce the price floor, and demonstrate a genuine commitment to the token's long-term value. This is the project putting capital back where it matters — into $AZY itself.

05

GIVEAWAYS

Community engagement is essential to $AZY's growth. A portion of fees funds giveaways and rewards for active community members, incentivizing participation and loyalty. Giveaways bring new eyes to the project while rewarding the holders who have been here from the start.

06

DEV ALLOCATION

A small percentage of creator fees is allocated to the developer for operational costs. In the interest of full transparency: the majority of this allocation is typically reinvested back into $AZY through purchasing additional supply on the open market — further strengthening the token and aligning the developer's interests directly with the community's.

Every fee supports the project.

$AZY's fee structure is designed with one principle: full reinvestment into the project and its community. From advertising and content to trending, buybacks, giveaways, and development — every allocation is made to strengthen $AZY for its holders.

There are no hidden agendas, no secret wallets. Just a clear, transparent commitment to using fees as a tool for growth and sustainability. This is how $AZY will continue to thrive — through strategic reinvestment and an unwavering focus on delivering value to its community.
