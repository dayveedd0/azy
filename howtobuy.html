HOW TO BUY 購入方法
$AZY on Solana — a step-by-step guide.

$AZYはSolanaブロックチェーン上のトークンです。以下の手順に従って購入できます。

$AZY is a token on the Solana blockchain. Follow the steps below to purchase. There are two main ways: through Jupiter (the largest Solana DEX aggregator) or through Pump.fun.

STEP 01 ウォレットの準備

Set up a Solana wallet

Solanaウォレットを作成する

Download and install a Solana-compatible wallet. The most popular options are:

  • Phantom — Available as a browser extension and mobile app. The most widely used Solana wallet.
  • Solflare — Another trusted option with browser and mobile support.

PhantomまたはSolflareをダウンロードし、ウォレットを作成してください。シードフレーズは安全な場所に保管してください。

Write down your seed phrase and store it somewhere safe. Never share it with anyone.

STEP 02 SOLの入手

Get SOL in your wallet

ウォレットにSOLを入金する

You need SOL (Solana's native token) to swap for $AZY. You can get SOL by:

  • Purchasing SOL on a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.) and sending it to your wallet address.
  • Using the built-in buy feature inside Phantom wallet (supports card payments in some regions).
  • Transferring SOL from another wallet you already own.

取引所でSOLを購入し、ウォレットアドレスに送金してください。ガス代として少額のSOLを残しておくことを推奨します。

Keep a small amount of SOL for transaction fees (usually less than $0.01 per transaction on Solana).

STEP 03-A Jupiter経由

Buy via Jupiter

Jupiterで購入する

Jupiter is the largest DEX aggregator on Solana. It finds the best price across all liquidity sources.

  1. Go to jup.ag
  2. Connect your Phantom or Solflare wallet (top-right corner).
  3. In the "You're paying" field, select SOL.
  4. In the "You're receiving" field, paste the $AZY contract address.
  5. Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap.
  6. Review the details and click "Swap".
  7. Approve the transaction in your wallet.

jup.agにアクセスし、ウォレットを接続してください。SOLを$AZYにスワップします。コントラクトアドレスを必ず確認してください。

Always verify the contract address before swapping. Only use the official contract address shared on our X account.

STEP 03-B Pump.fun経由

Buy via Pump.fun

Pump.funで購入する

Pump.fun is a Solana token launchpad where you can buy tokens directly.

  1. Go to pump.fun
  2. Search for $AZY or navigate directly using the token link (shared on our X).
  3. Connect your wallet.
  4. Enter the amount of SOL you want to spend.
  5. Click "Buy" and approve the transaction in your wallet.

pump.funにアクセスし、$AZYを検索してください。ウォレットを接続し、購入したいSOLの量を入力して購入を完了します。

Pump.fun is straightforward and beginner-friendly. Ideal for first-time buyers.

STEP 04 確認

Confirm your purchase

購入を確認する

After the transaction is complete, your $AZY tokens will appear in your wallet. You can verify by:

  • Checking your token balance in Phantom or Solflare.
  • Viewing the transaction on solscan.io or explorer.solana.com.

取引完了後、$AZYトークンがウォレットに表示されます。Solscanで取引を確認できます。

If $AZY doesn't appear immediately, you may need to add the token manually using the contract address.

注意 SAFETY

安全に取引するために、以下の点にご注意ください。

Always double-check that you are using the correct contract address. Only trust links shared on the official AZY X account. Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone. Be aware that cryptocurrency investments carry risk.

公式Xアカウントで共有されたリンクのみを信頼してください。シードフレーズや秘密鍵は絶対に他人と共有しないでください。
